Projects

RHS App

A school companion app for schedules, maps, news, and context-aware assistance around the Richmond Secondary day.

Schedule. See today's rotation, class blocks, rooms, teachers, and realtime updates in one glance.

School map. Search rooms and landmarks with a visual school map built for quick navigation between blocks.

Siri AI Integration. Your schedule is automatically indexed to Siri AI on iOS 27, allowing it to surface relevant information privately.

News and Updates. Browse, search and sort news articles without digging through scattered pages.

HSR Battlegrounds

A multiplayer 2D battleground inspired by Brawlhalla and Super Smash Bros, featuring teacher-inspired characters with unique abilities.

Shader-driven UI. Utilizes GLSL to create stunning user interface animations.

Unique mechanics. Distinct abilities and playstyles for each character, granting players diverse strategic options.

Feels like home. Stages and design elements are crafted to feel familiar to the school environment.

Gameplay. Mechanics inspired by games you know.

What We Do

We help students turn programming interest into real work: learning the fundamentals, building useful school projects, and testing skills through contests, game jams, and collaborative challenges.

Learn

Structured workshops, peer feedback, and lessons in Python, C++, Swift, responsible AI use, and the habits behind steady technical progress.

Build

Contribute code, interface design, testing, and planning on projects like the RHS Companion App, HSR Battlegrounds, and tools for other clubs. You will also be learning to use industry standard software and workflows like Git+Github, Xcode, Visual Studio Code, Figma, Blender and more.

Compete

Practice for CCC-style programming contests, game jams, hackathons, and esports collaborations that sharpen problem-solving under real constraints.
